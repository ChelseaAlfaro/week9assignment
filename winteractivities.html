Winter Activites

Mount Charleston and Lee Canyon are very popular during the winter due to the heavy snow and numerous activites available to locals and tourists. Below are some activites that are available during the winter months.

Skiing

Lee Canyon is a very popular skiing and snow boarding desitination. Passes can be purchased for the whole season or just for the day. There are lifts to the top of the runs and there are runs available for every level of experience. There are also classes available for both children and adults.

Horseback Riding

Horseback riding is available at Mount Charleston year round but is particularly fun during the winter months. There are multiple stables and companies that offer day rides over the trails on the mountain.

Snow Shoeing

There are new snowshoeing activites that are set to launch soon, but snowshoeing the numerous easy trails throughout the mountain is a popular choice for people looking to take in beautiful winter views.
